Connect is an open-source organizing tool that gives volunteers and staff a way to communicate and collaborate from anywhere.

Created with basic grassroots organizing principles in mind, Connect gives organizers a platform to make their work more accessible and more effective.

Features

Groups

Connect makes it easy to speak to the right people, at the right moment. Create unique groups for local, national, or issue-specific organizing.

Messages

Send and receive messages within groups, or right from your Connect inbox. Manage messages, groups, and subscription settings in a profile that's easy to customize.

Resources

Users, groups, and staff can host and share documents and best practices. Organizers should always have the most up-to-date information right at their fingertips.
